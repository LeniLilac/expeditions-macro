# Expeditions Macro v1.1.6

## Highlights

- Detects the live bottom-left **Play** control after a Challenge victory instead of using a fixed click coordinate, then verifies the screen transition and retries safely when Roblox misses the click.
- Ships detector pack 1.0.2 with all five Challenge map references and upgrades stale older installations automatically.
- Repairs a corrupted or mismatched same-version detector pack while preserving a genuinely newer installed pack.
- Reports detector-pack identity and Challenge capability in diagnostic ZIPs, and stops early with an actionable update message when Challenge recognition is unavailable.

## Notes

Existing camera models, placement models, Expedition presets, and Challenge presets remain compatible. Detector pack 1.0.2 is bundled and is also available as a separate release asset.
