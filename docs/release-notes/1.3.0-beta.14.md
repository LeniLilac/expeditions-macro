# Expeditions Macro v1.3.0-beta.14

This silent beta improves saved-team navigation and prevents rapid coarse camera input from turning a short correction into a nearly complete fallback scan. It does not send a Discord release announcement.

## Saved teams

- Unit Teams navigation now detects and holds the live gray scrollbar thumb instead of wheel-scrolling over unit cards.
- Every opening is verified at the reset-to-top position before the requested absolute alignment is applied.
- Teams 1 through 6 align to the first fully visible row. Teams 7 and 8 share the bottom limit and use their independently detected Load Team buttons.
- Load actions require consecutive aligned frames and a complete green button, preventing clicks on clipped rows or hover popups.

## Camera alignment

- Coarse yaw correction now sends at most six arrow pulses before re-observing the saved yaw atlas.
- Each observation recalculates the shortest remaining direction, correcting timing-dependent overshoot without committing to a 72-step scan.
- Ambiguous, stationary, cyclic, or exhausted feedback remains bounded and hands control to the existing verified refinement/fallback path.
- Manual Auto Align remains independent of the device-local spawn shortcut and retains direct scoring plus three-frame final verification.

## Validation

- Added a 72-step regression reproducing the reported beta.13 rapid-pulse overshoot and proving the goal is reached in under half a turn without entering full-turn fallback.
- Added a stationary-atlas regression proving closed-loop correction stops safely before the existing fallback.
- Added reviewed Team 1 through 8 alignment fixtures plus complete saved-team load regressions for the current and older Unit Teams layouts.
- The complete 571-test application/detector suite and all 5 Deep Debug Viewer tests pass.
- Repository policy, the warning-free Release build, 22-page packaged UI smoke test, isolated installer smoke test, and release-asset verification pass.
