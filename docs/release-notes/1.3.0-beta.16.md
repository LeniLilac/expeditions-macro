# Expeditions Macro v1.3.0-beta.16

This silent beta fixes Challenge daily-limit scheduling, saved-team loading for Teams 7 and 8, and current wide Challenge Defeat panels. It does not send a Discord release announcement.

## Challenge scheduling

- Challenge cooldown evidence now remains available across one-match Macro scheduler handoffs.
- If every regular Challenge is still unavailable after a complete global half-hour reset, the account's daily limits are treated as exhausted until the next `00:00 UTC`.
- The Challenge task stays ineligible during that period while the scheduler continues with the next highest-priority eligible task.

## Saved teams

- Team 7 and Team 8 still share the verified bottom scrollbar position and use their detected Load Team actions.
- Their visible confirmation dialogs are now recognized when the clicked or current row dims one underlying green action.
- Modal recognition continues to require multiple visible team rows, the dark confirmation structure, and an independently detected Confirm action.

## Match-end detection

- Current wide Challenge Defeat panels are recognized even when their dim red outer decoration joins the bright title banner.
- Recognition still requires the independent yellow **Repeat Stage** action.
- The obsolete small-Play-button post-match state was removed. The configured Play key opens navigation directly while Victory or Defeat remains visible.

## Validation

- All 612 application and detector tests pass, including the complete cross-state corpus and the reported Team 7/8 and current Defeat fixtures.
- All 5 Deep Debug Viewer tests pass.
- Repository policy, release metadata, the warning-free Release build, UI snapshots, packaged portable startup, installer startup/uninstall, and release-asset verification pass.
