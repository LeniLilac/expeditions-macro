# Expeditions Macro v1.3.0-beta.7

This silent beta improves camera startup and makes Story, Raid, Expedition, and Challenge navigation resilient across both lobby and post-match interfaces. It does not send a Discord release announcement.

## Camera alignment

- Normal macro runs learn a device-local shortcut after two matching load-in observations, then try one cached relative mouse drag on later matching runs.
- The shortcut is accepted only after three fresh goal captures pass; any mismatch falls back to the complete precision-preserving yaw atlas.
- Manual Auto Align remains independent of the learned shortcut, and stale shortcuts are automatically relearned or removed with their camera model.

## Navigation and scheduling

- Cross-mode handoffs press the configured Play key directly from Victory or Defeat, then use the detected Change Gamemode action to reach the shared Play selector.
- Story and Raid mode-detail detection now supports both lobby and post-match variants, including the full-width post-match Select Stage rail and the shifted lobby Story panel.
- Raid act selection no longer stalls when an existing party omits the Enter Matchmaking button.

## Engineering

- Added reviewed lobby and post-match fixtures for Story, Raid, Challenge, and Expedition mode-detail screens.
- Added repository policy enforcement and focused development, testing, game-behavior, and release documentation.

## Validation

- Release solution builds without warnings.
- The complete local Release suite and checked-in image regressions pass, including camera shortcut safety and all eight mode-detail variants.
- Release preflight, repository policy, checksums, native dependency checks, and portable-package smoke verification pass before tagging.
